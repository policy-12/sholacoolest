A Poem About Life

The world we live
The journey undefined
Everyday like an onion bulb
The more you peal a layer
The more we shed tears
Other time its like a roller coaster
Up there in the mountain today, down in the valley tomorrow
Other time we live like an outcast

List of life Challenges

  • Unemployement
  • Barreness
  • Marital issues
Life serve everyone its own dishes
Some meal so interesting, others so sour
Many taste it with a bud dead
No one have knowledge of his/her content
we just get served
Whatever life dishes out for you, please make it a moment
to savor and be stronger while you breath
Because no one is coming out alive
